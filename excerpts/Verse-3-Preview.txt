Hun main taareyan nu puchda questioning the sky,
Kyun tu kitti be-imani telling a lie,
Mere pyaar da mazaak tu making a joke,
Sade waadeyan da dhuaan leaving just smoke.
Par nasha teri aakh da holding me down,
Main taan aape nu lutawan losing my crown.
Teri zehar di aadat ne made me so blind,
Main sab kuch hi haareya losing my mind.
