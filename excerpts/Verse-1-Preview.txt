Guffe vargi is thand vich feeling the heat,
Main kalla raat nu kadd'da finding my feet,
Tu naina ton pilaundi ae holding the throne,
Tere husan da zehar chadeya chilling my bone.
Ni tu mithi jehi maut ae taking a hold,
Main jaan luttan nu phirda being so bold.
Sari raat main jagda aan watching your eyes,
Teri mithi jehi aawaz ch beautiful lies.
