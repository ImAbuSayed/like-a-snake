Ho tu zehar wangu chardi ae deep in my veins,
Main taan hosh sab bhulda taking the pains,
Ni tu kaale jehe libaas vich looking so fine,
Mere seene ch utardi ae crossing the line.
Sari duniya nu bhul gaya just for your face,
Tera kaatil jeha nakhra stunning the place,
Main taan mitti vich rull gaya burning so bright,
Tu te rooh vich utardi ae like a snake in the night.
